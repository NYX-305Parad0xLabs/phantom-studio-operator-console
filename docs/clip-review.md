# Clip Review

The review page surfaces viral moment candidates with the rationale, timestamps, transcript excerpts, and score so operators can make informed editorial decisions.

- **Candidate cards** show start/end seconds, virality score, transcript excerpt, style notes, and a best-clip badge when applicable.
- **Preview panel** offers a placeholder video area plus clip metadata for the selected card.
- **Compare mode** lets operators include up to three clips via checkboxes and reason about their ordering.
- **Actions** include accepting a candidate, requesting alternates, or marking ordering for a short series.
- **Transcript panel** confirms alignments with start/end timing and allows quick verification without leaving the review stage.
